🧮 How It's Calculated

The calculator uses the hypergeometric distribution — the same math as drawing colored balls from an urn without replacement.

P(X ≥ k) = 1 − ∑i=0k-1 C(K, i) × C(N − K, n − i) ⁄ C(N, n)
N
15 — atlas size (total sites)
K
6 — sources of element
n
5 — sites seen by this turn
k
1 — pips needed
✨ Combined Probability (Multi-Element)

For spells requiring two elements, the probabilities multiply — you need both to be met independently.
